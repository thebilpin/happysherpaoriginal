Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Why only weekend delivery?

We source fresh produce from Sydney Markets and coordinate delivery to maximize freshness while keeping costs affordable. Weekend delivery allows us to provide the best quality at competitive prices.

Where do items come from?

All our produce comes directly from Sydney Markets, Australia's premier fresh produce market. This ensures you get the freshest vegetables, fruits, and specialty items.

What is the minimum order?

We have a minimum order value to ensure delivery is economical. Check the app for current minimums in your delivery area.

Can I get Nepali and Asian vegetables?

Yes! We specialize in hard-to-find Nepali, Indian, and Asian vegetables sourced from Sydney Markets' specialty wholesalers.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive order confirmations and delivery updates via the app and SMS. You can track your order status in real-time through the Happy Sherpa app.
